General Information

The Callao is one of the most important port regions in Peru. It is known for its great cultural diversity, its history, and its significant commercial activity.

Location

The Callao is located on the central western coast of Peru, next to Lima, facing the Pacific Ocean.

Importance

It is the main port of Peru and one of the most important ports in South America. It facilitates international trade and plays a key role in the country's economy.

Tourism

The Callao has touristic places such as La Punta, Real Felipe Fortress, and the Naval Museum. Visitors can enjoy beaches, historical sites, and local cuisine.
